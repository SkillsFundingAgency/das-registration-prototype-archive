Add a PAYE scheme

We need you to add the PAYE scheme for your organisation.

You can do this by using either of the following:

  • the Government Gateway login for your organisation
  • your accounts office reference number for your organisation

Find your accounts office and PAYE scheme reference numbers

You can find your 13-character accounts office reference number on the letter HMRC sent you when you first registered as an employer.

You will find your employer PAYE scheme reference in the payslip booklet HMRC provided. Contact HMRC or your accountant if you do not have access to the booklet.
